Just One

A minimalist habit tracker. Start with one habit. Build from there.

Features

🔥

Streak Tracking

Build weekly streaks and see your consistency grow over time. One missed day doesn't have to end it — use Streak Savers to protect your progress.

📊

Visual Heatmaps

GitHub-style contribution graphs show weeks of history at a glance. Drag across dates for detailed day-by-day breakdowns.

🚀

Progressive Journeys

Set a start and goal value, and level up over time. Great for reading, running, meditation — anything you want to build gradually.

🚫

Break Habits

Track habits you're quitting. Count clean days, log slip-ups, and build streaks of resistance.

📱

Home Screen Widgets

Check off habits right from your Home Screen or Lock Screen without opening the app.

🛡️

Streak Savers

Fill in a missed day so one slip doesn't erase weeks of progress. Available as tokens or unlimited with Pro.
